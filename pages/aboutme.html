About Me




I graduated from AIEP Institute in Chile with a bachelor's degree in 3D Animation. I completed my internship at Torque Ingenieria for 3 months as a 3D modeler and Animator.


I have a passion for modeling props and environment and love when I get the opportunity to combine the two. I have always admired animated films and can't wait to be a part of making them!


My experience spans the entire development pipeline, working closely with design, engineering, and production to ensure assets meet quality standards while remaining performant and scalable. I understand how to deliver work that not only looks great but also integrates cleanly into real-time environments.
