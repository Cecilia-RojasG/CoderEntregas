HIGHLIGHTED


These models were created for use in an animation about safety in offices and mining work sites. Everything was created from scratch, including the settings, props, and characters.

Mudbox and 3D Maya were used for modeling. The textures were created and applied using the Substance suite.

Post-production was done in Photoshop and Premiere
