Mine Workers


Made in Maya 3D and Mudbox from images and data provided by the client so that the elements correspond to those used in reality. No use of AI.
The project needed two characters to show the contrast between a worker who was incorrectly equipped and performing reckless actions, and another who was correctly equipped and protected.
